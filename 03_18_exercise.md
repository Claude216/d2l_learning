### sub1:

- f(b) = $\sum_i^n (x_i - b)^2$, then $\frac {d} {db} f(b) = \sum (-2) (x_i - b) = 0$ 

- $\sum (x_i - b) = 0 \rarr \sum x_i - \sum b = 0 \rarr \sum x_i - nb = 0$

- Thus, $b = \frac {1} {n} \sum x_i$
